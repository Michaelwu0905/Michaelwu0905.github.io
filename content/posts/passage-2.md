# IELTS Writing Task 2
## Question
The money spent by governments on space programmes would be better spent on vital public services such as schoolss and hospitals.

To what extent do you agree or disagree.

## Example
Some people believe that the government money spent on space programs should instead be used for essential public services, such as schools and hospitals. I partly agree with this view because education and healthcare should clearly be priorities, but I also believe that space exploration should continue to receive a reasonable level of funding.

On the one hand, public services are more closely connected with people's daily lives. Schools provide children with knowledge and skills, while hospitals protect people's health and save lives. If these services are underfunded, the whole society may suffer. For example, poor schools may produce less qualified workers in the future, and overcrowded hospitals may fail to treat patients quickly. Therefore, when a government has limited budget, it should first make sure that basic services are properly funded.

On the other hand, this does not mean that space programmes are a waste of money. Space research can bring important long-term benefits. For instance, satellites are used for weather forecasting, global communication, navigation and disaster monitoring. These technologies are useful in ordinary life and can also help governments deal with serious problems such as storms, floods and and climate change. In addition, space projects can encourage young people to study science and engineering, which may benefit a country's future development.

In conclusion, I partly agree that schools and hospitals should receive more attention from governments, especially when they lack sufficient funding. However, space programmes should not be completely abandoned, as they can bring scientific, practical and educational benefits. A balanced approach is therefore the best solution.

## 错误总结
1. 时态，议论文一般不会使用过去式，采用一般现在时。
2. 不要偷换概念，原题限定了public services和space programmes的对立关系，就不要偷换概念或采用其二级概念，如将space programmes偷换成scientific experiment，public services用teachers（二级概念）代指等。
3. 观点的衔接，在brainstorm阶段可以无序地列出自己的想法和观点，但最后要想好先后顺序，注意观点之间的逻辑和衔接关系。
4. 用书面词汇和语法，积累好的词汇和语法。
5. 注意字数不少于250词
